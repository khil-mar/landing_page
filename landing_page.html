About us

We are a team of students from IITM Techfest, IIT Bombay is the annual science and technology festival of the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. Techfest originated in 1998 to provide a platform for the Indian student community to develop and showcase their technical prowess. It has grown into Asia's Largest Science and Technology Festival, attracting people from all walks of life, including students, academia, professionals, and the general public.
